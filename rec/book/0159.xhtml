In 1917, two young girls from Cottingley, England, released a set of photographs supposedly showing fairies they had seen in their garden. The images were fake—the fairies were simply paper cutouts—but people around the world believed the Cottingley Fairies were real. In 2018, the original photos sold at auction for over $25,000. 159
