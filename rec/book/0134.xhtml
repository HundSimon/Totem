READING SKILL Understanding Claims Many articles and scientific texts cite research or expert opinions to support claims put forth by the writer. One way to understand the strength of a claim is to look closely at the verbs used. Verbs such as find (out), point out, know, discover, and conclude show a high degree of confidence in the claims being presented. Verbs such as suggest, think, believe, and claim show a lower degree of confidence. SCANNING A. Look back at Reading A. Find and underline the claims below. 1.
