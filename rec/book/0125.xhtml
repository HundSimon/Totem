READING SKILL Taking Notes on a Reading (2)—Using a Concept Map An alternative way of taking notes on a reading is by using a concept map. A concept map can help organize information in a more memorable way. If the reading is about a specific person or thing, a common way to organize a concept map is to start with a circle containing the person or thing’s name. Around the circle, note the main ideas that the reading covers, and, finally, the key details. Draw lines to connect the ideas. TAKING NOTES A.
