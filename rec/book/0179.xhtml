READING SKILL Understanding Definitions in a Text A reading may include words that are defined in footnotes, or defined in the text itself. When a word is defined in a text, its definition may follow a comma or dash (—), or be set off by two commas or dashes. Look for these definitions as you read to better comprehend a text. Examples: Many smaller planes are powered by a propeller, a device with blades that spin at high speed. Future planes will need less thrust—the force from the engine that moves it forward.
