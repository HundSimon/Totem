Two of the most popular types of rose plants are the hybrid tea and the floribunda. As cut flowers, they sell in large numbers around the world, and the plants are also commonly grown in people’s gardens. While similar in appearance, the two flowers have a number of differences. The hybrid tea has large, single flowers on each stem, whereas floribundas produce groups of smaller flowers on a single stem. The hybrid tea plant is also taller than the floribunda.
